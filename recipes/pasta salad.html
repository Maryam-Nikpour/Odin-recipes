Pasta Salad

Description

Pasta salad is a type of salad that consists of cooked pasta, often combined with other ingredients such as vegetables, cheese, meats, or dressing. The ingredients are typically chilled before serving, creating a refreshing and satisfying dish that can be served as a main course or as a side dish. Pasta salad can be customized to suit individual tastes, making it a versatile and popular dish.
